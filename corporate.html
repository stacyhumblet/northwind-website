Operations / Reporting

Eliminating 12 Hours of Weekly Manual Reporting

Challenge

A regional distribution company's finance team spent 12+ hours every week manually pulling data from three separate systems, reformatting in Excel, and emailing reports to leadership — with no guarantee of accuracy or consistency.

Solution

Northwind built an automated data consolidation pipeline feeding a Power BI dashboard with a daily scheduled refresh. Leadership now opens a live report each morning instead of waiting for a manually compiled email.

12 hrs saved per week
Daily automated refresh
3 → 1 systems consolidated
Workflow Automation

Client Onboarding Reduced from 4 Days to Same Day

Challenge

A professional services firm managed client onboarding through manual email chains, approval requests, and document handoffs. The process took 3–4 business days and regularly fell through the cracks when staff were busy.

Solution

Northwind designed and implemented an end-to-end onboarding workflow using Microsoft Power Automate — handling approvals, document generation, notifications, and system updates automatically from a single form submission.

4d → 1d onboarding time
Zero missed steps
100% process consistency
